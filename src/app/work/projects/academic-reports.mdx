## Overview

This report portfolio captures a broad range of analytical topics in economics and finance, including market behavior, macro indicators, commodity dynamics, and valuation. The work reflects structured reasoning and communication across different problem types.

## Objective

- Build analytical depth across real-world economic and financial questions.
- Demonstrate consistent method selection based on problem context.
- Present insights clearly for non-technical and technical readers.

## Data / Inputs

- Eight academic reports covering poverty, HDI, commodity price changes, policy communication, market simulation, weather prediction, valuation, and property market analysis.

## Methodology

- Statistical and comparative analysis.
- Variable and driver assessment.
- Scenario interpretation and narrative synthesis.
- Report structuring for evidence-based conclusions.

## Key Findings

- Clear variable framing materially improves interpretation quality in macro and socioeconomic analysis.
- Cross-domain research skills transfer well into investment due diligence and sector analysis.
- Strong narrative discipline increases decision usefulness of quantitative work.

## Business Impact

These reports demonstrate the foundation for high-quality investment research: hypothesis clarity, disciplined analysis, and recommendation-ready writing.

## Tools Used

- Academic and market datasets
- Spreadsheet-based analysis
- Report writing and data storytelling
