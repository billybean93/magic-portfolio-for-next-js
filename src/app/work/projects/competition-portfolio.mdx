## Overview

This competition portfolio demonstrates practical application of finance and analytics under time pressure, team collaboration constraints, and judged delivery contexts.

## Objective

- Convert data and business context into persuasive recommendations.
- Balance analytical rigor with presentation clarity.
- Deliver finalist-level outputs in competitive settings.

## Data / Inputs

- RMIT Research Challenge submissions and certificate.
- RMIT Business Analytics Champion submissions and certificate.

## Methodology

- Problem framing and KPI selection.
- Valuation and comparative analysis.
- BI-driven diagnostics and recommendation development.
- Narrative-focused synthesis for judges.

## Key Findings

- Integrated finance + analytics framing improved recommendation quality.
- Team workflow discipline was critical for high-volume analysis and tight deadlines.
- Finalist outcomes reflect strong translation from analysis to decision-oriented messaging.

## Business Impact

These projects validate readiness for analyst roles requiring fast learning, structured judgment, and concise communication.

## Tools Used

- Business analytics techniques
- Financial analysis frameworks
- Presentation and stakeholder communication
