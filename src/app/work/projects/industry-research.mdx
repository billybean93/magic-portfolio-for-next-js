## Overview

This project evaluates opportunities in edtech and proptech through a structured market research lens. It combines industry trend analysis, business model benchmarking, and quantitative support materials to identify where growth potential aligns with investment criteria.

## Objective

- Build a practical sector thesis for early-stage opportunity screening.
- Assess market attractiveness and unit-level operating dynamics.
- Translate findings into investment-oriented recommendations.

## Data / Inputs

- Edtech research report and supporting calculation workbook.
- Proptech market research report.
- Public benchmarks and comparable market signals used in report assumptions.

## Methodology

- Top-down and bottom-up market framing.
- Business model decomposition by value chain and monetization logic.
- Competitive and funding landscape review.
- Key metric interpretation tied to growth and defensibility.

## Key Findings

- Edtech opportunities are strongest where outcomes can be measured and pricing aligns with clear ROI.
- Proptech opportunities show stronger potential when paired with operational process improvement rather than listing-only models.
- Market narrative quality improves significantly when accompanied by structured supporting calculations.

## Business Impact

The deliverables provide reusable market-intelligence artifacts for deal triage, founder conversations, and investment committee preparation.

## Tools Used

- Excel
- Secondary market research frameworks
- Structured comparative analysis
